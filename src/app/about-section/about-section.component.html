About

I was born in Minnesota, but because of my father's work, I was raised in about four different states. Since I moved around a lot, it was tough keeping friends in school, so I figured that making friends online would be more suited for my lifestyle.

What I didn't know was that being online would begin this love and passion for technology and software. It also allowed me to develop deep connections with anyone from anywhere.

I would have never guessed that my hobbies and love for technology would lead me to where I am today. I am always in awe at the beautiful and powerful technological solutions that brilliant engineers and developers come up with and I aspire to also make a positive impact on the world through my skills, effort, and passion.
